About

My name is Symeon Sideris. I was born and live in Thessaloniki.
Since 2014 I undertake complete IT solutions such as Websites, e-Shops, Custom Apps, Audio / Video Streaming, Mobile Applications.

Symeon Sideris
Full Stack Developer (Programmer)
"Gabrieli Media Group" Collaborator

In 2016 I received a degree as an Electrical Installation Assistant, Networks and Systems from the 2nd Vocational High School of Evosmos (Thessaloniki).

I continued my studies at ATEI Thessaloniki as an Automation Engineer and now I am in the 5th year of undergraduate studies in the new university department of Production (Industrial) Engineering and Management of IHU. Along with my studies I was chosen to be in one student research team of my school dealing with ROS (Robotic Operating System), Autonomous Vehicles and robotics at a more general level.

In 2019 I undertook project of AUTh entitled: "Development and support of information systems for large volume management data and data presentation applications ". In addition, I collaborated with LASCM (AUTh laboratory) in Industrial Informatics.

Currently I have the pleasure of working with Gabrieli Media Group .
At the same time, I do shows on webradios since 2010, finding my "radio house" at European School Radio where I am a member of the Board of Directors and since 2020 I am on the project team of the Erasmus+ "Social Radio 2.0" program through which will be build an innovative podcasting, radio and community platform for schools in Greece and Europe.
