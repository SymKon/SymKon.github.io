24/7 Sports Webradio

The mini website is created with simple HTML and CSS.
There is an HTML5 player supported on each system (computers, tablets, smartphones)
The webradio is based on the Icecast Server it manages through Centova Panel
It is configured to broadcast another webradio 24/7 and also live broadcasts directly from the server of Sfina.gr
There is also the possibility of an automatic dj that gives you the opportunity upload your own music and stream it with specific automation conditions
